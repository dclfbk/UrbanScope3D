# Internship Training Plan

## Title
Urban Scope 3D\
3D Visualization Prototype for Integrating Environmental Data in Urban Contexts

## Educational Objectives

* Acquire skills in the management and 3D visualization of geospatial data
* Understand the integration between 3D urban models and environmental data (e.g., temperature and solar radiation)
* Apply computational methods for solar position calculation and urban shadow simulation
* Develop skills in modular software design within a web-based environment
* Work with real-world datasets related to European urban innovation projects

## Activities

Development of a 3D urban mapping prototype with integrated environmental data.

In particular:

* Analysis and preprocessing of urban datasets (buildings, vegetation, terrain)
* Implementation of 3D visualization of the urban environment using dedicated frameworks (e.g., CesiumJS, Three.js, or similar)
* Integration of a module for solar position calculation
* Implementation of shadow simulation based on date and time
* Integration of informational layers related to urban temperature
* Structuring and documenting the codebase in a modular way

## Expected Skills

By the end of the internship, the student will have developed competencies in:

* 3D computer graphics applied to urban contexts
* Management and integration of geospatial data
* Design and development of interactive web applications
* Computational modeling of solar geometry
* Software project organization and documentation

Additionally, transversal skills such as problem solving and technical presentation will be strengthened.

## Evaluation Methods

Learning outcomes will be assessed through:

* **Periodic monitoring meetings**
  During which the student will present progress updates, discuss technical solutions, and address any challenges encountered

* **Evaluation of developed outputs**
  With particular attention to the correct functioning of the prototype, the coherence of the software architecture, and the quality of dataset integration

* **Code and documentation review**
  Considering clarity, modularity, reusability, and proper explanation of methodological choices

## Work Modality

Hybrid mode (on-site and remote), with regular meetings for coordination and progress monitoring.
